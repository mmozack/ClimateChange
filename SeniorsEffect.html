How will this Impact Me?

Heat

  • Impact of exposure to extreme heat varies depending on housing structures and communities to accommodate hot climate conditions
  • Individual health factors interact with exposure to heat:
    Pre-existing chronic health conditions such as cardiovascular are susceptible to heat exposure.

Severe Storms

  • Climate change will bring about more frequent hurricanes and severe storms that may require evacuation of facilities that provide long term care to seniors
  • Some seniors may consider evacuation too burdensome and instead stay in place until it is too late to evacuate.

Air Pollution

  • Fine particulate in the air may impact seniors more, particularly individuals with preexisting health issues specifically related to lung disease.
